Instructions
  • The rooms in the maze are symbolized by a 7x7 square grid cells. For each room we know that which wall has a door.
  • Click on the arrows to slide the row or column and insert the moving room
  • Left click on the moving room to rotate.
  • By cleverly sliding the rooms players try to find their way to the treasures. The first player to find all their treasures and return to the starting square is the winner.
